Milestones of the AuthDNA Research Project.

Project Proposal

Description: State what is the problem going to solve from the research project. This will describe What research question will be addressed, and how they will be addressed, how much time and expense will be required for the research, What prior research has been done on the topic, How the results of the research will be evaluated, how the research will benefit the sponsoring organization and other parties.

Marks allocated : 10%

Deadline : 14 March 2019

PPR Assessment

Description:Preliminary progress report of the research project. This will provide a brief look at preliminary findings or in-progress work on the project of technical manner. this will include, the purpose of the research, what are the aspects that the research covers, what are the technical objectives of the developing the research project, what technology will be used for the project, how do we collect test data and the how-to calculate results and the benefits of the research project.

Marks allocated : 6%

Deadline : 13 May 2019

Progress Presentation-1

Description: 50% of the project should be done including a working prototype of the project.

Marks allocated : 10%

Deadline : 24 Jun 2019

Progress Presentation-2

Description: 90% of the project should be done including a test results of the project.

Marks allocated : 18%

Deadline : 04 Sep 2019

Final Assessment

Description:Present the completed research project to the audience with testing results and findings. this will be asses the accuracy of the project, the competency of the project, how slove the research problems identified with the completed project.

Marks allocated : 10%

Deadline : 13-15 Nov 2019

Final Viva

Description:Q & A session for the research project asses the technical objectives, results, data set of the testing, and the completeness of the project.

Marks allocated : 15%

Deadline : 13-15 Nov 2019
